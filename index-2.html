Making A Difference

Holy Grace Academy of Management Studies in Thrissur started its journey in 2005 and has been consistently pursuing its mission of delivering excellence with great zeal and perseverance in the areas of management and research. The institution offers its flagship program, Masters in Business Administration, which is a comprehensive industry endorsed package drafted around the syllabus prescribed by the University of Calicut. Regarded as among the best MBA colleges in Kerala, the institute aligns programs to industry needs and offer numerous avenues for learning and exposure.
