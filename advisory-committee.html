  • DR. ABDUL SALAM

Dr. Abdul Salam: Former Vice Chancellor, University of Calicut, is a renowned educationist, internationally known agricultural scientist and a seasoned academic-administrator. In a career spanning more than four decades across four continents, Dr. M Abdul Salam has established a reputation as a top-end scientist-educationist, besides being recognized as one of the most authoritative voices in the area of higher education. With over 150 research papers and 13 books to his credit, Dr. Salam has served in various institutions in the capacities of Vice Chancellor, Researcher, Professor, Scientist and Administrator. The national and international awards and fellowships that have come in search of him include the Post-doctoral Commonwealth Academic Staff Fellowship, Krishi Vijnan Award, Vice President GS Pathak Prize, Ponnuswami Chetiar Medal and a host of other recognitions. His PhD thesis won four awards including the Foreign Examiners' Donation Award. Dr. Salam did his postdoctoral training and research in 1991 at the University of Aberdeen, Scotland (1991). Dr. Salam represents the academia in our Board.

  • PROF. K. RAVEENDRANATH

Prof. K. Raveendranath, A University rank-holder at the Master's Degree level in Commerce, Professor Raveendranath joined this institution after his four-year term in Calicut University as its Pro Vice Chancellor. He was chosen to this second highest position in the University while he was serving Shree Krishna College, Guruvayoor as its Principal. A teacher of undisputed excellence Prof. Raveendranath taught for 31 years which includes his 11 years experience in the PG Department. As a University top-brass, Prof Raveendranath was member of the University Syndicate, Senate, Academic Council, UGC Autonomy Committee for affiliated colleges, Commerce Board of Studies and Chairman of the Committee constituted by the Kerala State Higher Education Council to study issues related to the University and college Administrative staff. He also served as the Consultant Expert for the NAAC re-accreditation of Shree Krishna College Guruvayoor. His extension activities include his tenure as NSS Program Officer and associate Secretary of the Indian National Committee of World University Service.
